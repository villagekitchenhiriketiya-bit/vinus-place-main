Our Story

Where Tropical Warmth

Vinu's Place was born from a simple belief — that every traveller deserves more than just a room. They deserve a connection. To the place, the culture, the flavours, and most importantly, the people.

Nestled on Samagi Road in the heart of Hiriketiya, our family has been welcoming guests from around the world. From the independence of our apartment to the warmth of Lucky Homestay and the spice-filled journey at Village Kitchen — every experience carries the same promise: authenticity.
